    PHARMACY

    Pharmacy, a multifaceted healthcare profession, revolves around the science and art of medication management. Pharmacists, the highly trained and specialized professionals in this field, serve as the gatekeepers of safe and effective drug use. Their primary mission is to optimize patient health outcomes through comprehensive medication therapy management. Pharmacy encompasses a wide spectrum of roles and responsibilities. In community pharmacies, pharmacists dispense prescription medications, offer over-the-counter remedies, and provide essential health advice to individuals. They are instrumental in ensuring the correct dosage, potential drug interactions, and patient education, all of which are crucial for medication safety. In hospitals and healthcare institutions, clinical pharmacists collaborate closely with healthcare teams to design and monitor intricate drug regimens, tailoring treatments to individual patient needs.
